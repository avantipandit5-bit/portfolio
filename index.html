About Me

I used to be afraid of code. Genuinely intimidated. Then AI-assisted development happened — Cursor, Lovable, Bolt, Streamlit. Suddenly I was building and deploying real products that impact daily lives.

When I started building ConnectAbility, something clicked. The roadmap, the user conversations, the prototype iterations — it all made sense. What began as a hackathon became 2nd Prize at Protothon 7.0. More than that, it became the moment I stopped wondering if PM was right for me.

Building is now a hobby. AI is my co-pilot. Four products live right now — all solving real problems for real people. Focused on AI-assisted products that impact daily lives.

Before this, Schneider Electric taught me what most early-career PMs never see: software failing at 2am across plants in 11 countries. I shipped a chatbot cutting ticket volume 40%, led a physical-to-AWS migration with zero downtime, and owned workflows for 1,400+ users. I did not manage these. I owned them.
